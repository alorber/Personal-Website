During my senior year of high school, I took my first programming course, AP Computer Science, and I was hooked. The course consisted of watching videos at home to learn the material and reviewing in class. The units of videos were released monthly and I would complete each month's work within the first few days. My teacher would give me other work to do, mainly consisting of Project Euler, that would allow me to learn more and practice the skills I had already learned.


Later that year, my teacher asked me if I would like to build a website for a school art project. I agreed and spent time teaching myself web development through online resources. I used my newly acquired skills to create a website for The Ramaz Artist Project. It was a great learning experience and my first attempt at web developpment.
Check out the project here!
